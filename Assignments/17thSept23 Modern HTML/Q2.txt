Q2 What are the html entities? List out 5 Commonly used entities.

Solution :
HTML entities are the special codes that Represents the some characters that have special meanings.
They often used to display reversed characters such as '<' or '>' as well as the special characters like ampersand (&)
examples :
1. &amp - Ampersand (&)
2. &lt - Less than (<)
3. &gt - Greater than (>)
4 &quot - Double quotation mark (")
5. &nbsp - Non-breaking space
